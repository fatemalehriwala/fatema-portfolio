About Me

Hi, I’m Fatema Mamajiwala – a purpose-driven and curious mind with a background that blends business, tech, and human insight. I hold a Bachelor’s in Commerce, but my growing interest in technology led me to pursue a Master’s in Computer Applications (MCA), where I explored everything from Java, Python, Android Development, DSA, to Data Warehousing, Cloud Computing, and Networking.

Throughout my academic journey, I tasted various tech domains—but what truly resonated with me was how data can drive decisions and how systems can be designed to solve real-world problems.

That curiosity brought me to EVERSANA, where I worked as a Full Stack Intern building scalable components using .NET, React, and MSSQL. I contributed to reusable UI components, backend data models, and API integrations—experiencing end-to-end development firsthand.

Later, I was offered a more strategic role at a startup in its early stages, where I worked as a Business Analyst. I collaborated on Jira-based sprint planning, wrote detailed documentation, managed tasks in Agile environments, created component architectures, and handled stakeholder communication using tools like Excel. Though the job lacked formal structure and long-term security, it helped me build confidence in cross-functional collaboration and product thinking.

Today, I’m channeling all those experiences into a focused goal: mastering Business Intelligence using SQL and Power BI. I'm currently building a real-world project — a Job & Skill Tracker Dashboard — that not only sharpens my technical skills but also solves a problem I deeply relate to as a job seeker.

What drives me? A hunger to learn, a need to make an impact, and a love for simplifying complex problems.
What makes me different? I’ve worn many hats—from coder to analyst—and I use that versatility to bridge gaps between people, systems, and decisions.
What am I looking for? A role where I can grow as a Data Analyst or Developer, contribute meaningfully, and transform data into decisions that matter.
